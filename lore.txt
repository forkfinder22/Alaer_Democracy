The President is the EEA candidate due to a united front between the SA, DPA, SCP, GA, BA, NLP, EEA, IA, and DP against the united front candidate of the Right, which coalition consisted of the ULCPA, NLPA, NCPA, AP.

Ideology naming:
(CPA):
Zhyetovism - Named for the legendary Bolshevik, Vladimir Lenin
Stasio-ism - Named for the dedicated Left Communist, Amedeo Bordiga
Mitterism - Named for the principled Conciliator, Huge Eberlein
(SU):
Tarragonism - Named for the prominent CNT-FAI, an anarcho-syndicalist federation that controlled Catalonia during the Spanish Civil War
Hallism - Named for the masterful IWW Songwriter, Joe Hill
Littinkism - Named for the inspiring Council Communist and Astronomer, Anton Pannekoek. Right-Syndicalism doesn't really have anything to do with Pannekoek, but he was an influential figure on the right-wing end of Communist Theory
O'Morranism - Named for the courageous James Connolly. Connolly would probably be a Hallist or a Tarragonist in this world, however.

Head of parliament decides the Coalition and ministers, but the parties negotiate for the ministries.
The Speaker doesn't do anything mechanically for the player, but it does provide 5 leverage. The largest party decides which delegate to make the speaker
